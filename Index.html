About This Project

The Manipuri New Word Bank is a collaborative initiative to document and preserve Manipuri (Meitei) vocabulary written in Bengali script, contributed by teachers, educators and language enthusiasts.

Each entry records the word in Bengali script, its first letter classification, its meaning in Assamese, and its English translation — building a multilingual reference for Manipuri learners.

Contributions are open to all educators and language professionals. Use the Add New Word button to submit a new word.
